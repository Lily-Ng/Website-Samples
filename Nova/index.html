Getting Started

Guide of the workflow from start to finish.

PROCESS


Step 1. Place Request
Browse for desired commission. Click on the "Order Now" button to submit a commission request. I will respond to your request shortly.
Step 2. Initial Sketch
A sketch will be sent after payment is received. You may request a work in progress at any part of the process.
Step 3. Modifications
You may request minor changes and revisions (color adjustments, small details, etc). Additional rates apply for significant changes.
Step 4. Final Piece
After confirming the work and all changes, you will receive large and resized versions of the artwork.
